# Role: Executive Sponsor

The Executive Sponsor focuses on high-level safety intelligence, ESG compliance, and organizational health.

**Total Pages Accessible: 6**

---

## 1. Executive Safety Intelligence Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** View high-level safety KPIs and leading indicators.
*   **Content:**
    *   **KPI Tiles:** Safety Score (0-100), Total Incidents (MTI/LTI), CAPA Closure Rate.
    *   **Charts:** Incident Trends (12 months), Compliance Heatmap by Region.
    *   **AI Insight:** Predictive risk forecast for the next 7 days.
*   **Primary Actions:** Export Board Report, Drill down into regional data.

## 2. AI Safety Intelligence Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor AI-driven predictive safety insights.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Predictive Risk Index:** High-risk work areas identified by AI.
    *   **Recommended Controls:** AI-suggested safety measures for current high-risk tasks.
    *   **Weekly Briefing:** AI-generated summary of platform activities.
*   **Primary Actions:** Approve Weekly Briefing, View AI model cards.

## 3. Compliance Score Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor ISO and regulatory compliance levels.
*   **Content:**
    *   **ISO Clause Coverage:** Progress against ISO 45001/14001.
    *   **Non-Conformance List:** Critical findings across all sites.
*   **Primary Actions:** Export Compliance Evidence.

## 4. Incident Analytics Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Deep dive into incident patterns.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Hotspot Map:** Geographic distribution of near-misses and incidents.
    *   **Severity Analysis:** Ratio of minor vs. major events.
*   **Primary Actions:** View investigation status.

## 5. Knowledge Library (Web)
*   **Purpose:** Review corporate HSE policies.
*   **Content:** Corporate Policies, Standards, and Lessons Learned.

## 6. Global Search
*   **Purpose:** Find any record, user, or document across the tenant.
