# Role: Safety Manager

The Safety Manager is the operational owner of safety processes, focusing on compliance, permits, and incident response.

**Total Pages Accessible: 12**

---

## 1. Site HSE Command Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Live overview of site safety status.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Live Board:** Active Permits, Pending Approvals, Expiring Work.
    *   **Site Metrics:** Open Hazards, Unassigned CAPAs, Site Safety Score.
*   **Primary Actions:** Bulk approve permits, Assign hazards.

## 2. Permit Live Board
*   **Purpose:** Monitor and manage all high-risk work in real-time.
*   **Content:** Permit list, Zone conflict alerts, Equipment lock-out status.
*   **Primary Actions:** Resolve conflicts, Extend/Close permits.

## 3. Incident Analytics & Investigation Workspace
*   **Purpose:** Classify and investigate safety events.
*   **Content:** Detailed reports, RCA tools (5-Why), Evidence gallery (Photos).
*   **Primary Actions:** Classify severity, Start investigation, Generate CAPA.

## 4. Risk Register Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor the site risk profile.
*   **Content:** Risk matrix (5x5), List of high-risk tasks, Status of control measures.
*   **Primary Actions:** Create Risk Assessment, Update Control Measures.

## 5. Audit and CAPA Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Track internal audits and corrective actions.
*   **Content:** Audit schedule, Findings list, CAPA aging report.
*   **Primary Actions:** Schedule audit, Review finding, Approve CAPA closure.

## 6. Training Compliance Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Ensure workforce readiness.
*   **Content:** Departmental heatmap, Overdue certification list.
*   **Primary Actions:** Export gap report, Send training reminders.

## 7. Vendor Compliance Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Manage contractor safety status.
*   **Content:** Approved/Suspended list, Expiring insurance documents.
*   **Primary Actions:** Review vendor documents, Suspend non-compliant vendor.

## 8. Asset Compliance Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor equipment safety.
*   **Content:** Inspection schedule, Overdue machinery audits.
*   **Primary Actions:** View inspection history.

## 9. Notification and Escalation Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor system alerts.
*   **Content:** Failed notification log, Active escalation rules.

## 10. Knowledge Usage Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Monitor SOP adoption.
*   **Content:** Acknowledgement rates per department.

## 11. My Tasks Dashboard
*   **Purpose:** Focus on assigned work.

## 12. Mobile Home (Mobile App)
*   **Purpose:** Field access to all core safety functions.
